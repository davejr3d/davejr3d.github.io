A solo project I created during an open brief over four months as my final major university project. Using Blender and Substance for 3D, Nuke for compositing and edited in Davinci.

I focused on capturing quiet, atmospheric moments of people simply existing in the rain. With no strict narrative, the project focuses on Ambience, lighting, and composition to evoke a sense of comfort while embracing quiet stillness.

Each scene is its own moment and glimpse into an individual experience, connected only by the presence of rain and the feeling it brings.
